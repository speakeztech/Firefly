The Commercial License for Firefly provides:

- **Patent Rights**: Explicit patent license covering the Fidelity Framework technology, including the "System and Method for Zero-Copy Inter-Process Communication Using BARE Protocol" (US Patent Application No. 63/786,247)

Firefly Commercial Licenses are available in several tiers:

### Standard Commercial License

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need a Commercial License?**

A: You need a Commercial License if you are using Firefly in a commercial product or service, if you want commercial support, or if you need to distribute Firefly as part of a proprietary solution that you do not want to open source.

**Q: Can I try Firefly before purchasing a Commercial License?**

A: Yes, you can evaluate Firefly under the Apache License 2.0 for a limited period before deciding to purchase a Commercial License.

**Q: What happens when my Commercial License expires?**

A: You will need to renew your license to continue using Firefly commercially. Without renewal, you would need to transition to using Firefly under the Apache License 2.0 and comply with all its terms.

**Q: Can I upgrade from one license tier to another?**

A: Yes, you can upgrade your license tier at any time. The cost difference will be prorated based on the remaining term of your current license.

**Q: Does the Firefly Commercial License cover the entire Fidelity Framework?**

A: The Firefly Commercial License covers Firefly itself. The Fidelity Framework includes other components (Alloy, BAREWire, Farscape) which are separately licensed but available under similar terms. Contact us for bundled licensing options.
